# CRM UI Requirements

## CRM Navigation

CRM menu should include:

- Dashboard.
- Accounts.
- Contacts.
- Leads.
- Opportunities.
- Activities.
- Reports.

## CRM Dashboard

Dashboard should show:

- Pipeline value.
- Weighted pipeline.
- Deals closing this month.
- Stale opportunities.
- Opportunities by stage chart.
- Latest activities.
- Follow-ups due today.

## Account List

Columns:

- Account name.
- Type.
- Status.
- Owner.
- Open opportunities.
- Pipeline value.
- Last activity.

## Account Detail

Tabs:

- Overview.
- Contacts.
- Opportunities.
- Quotes.
- Projects.
- Activities.
- Documents.
- Audit.

## Opportunity Board

Kanban board grouped by stage.

Cards should show:

- Opportunity name.
- Account.
- Value.
- Probability.
- Expected close date.
- Owner.
- Alert indicator.

## Opportunity Detail

Sections:

- Summary.
- Commercials.
- Contacts.
- Activities.
- Quotes.
- Pre-sales requests.
- Documents.
- History.

Sprint 5.1 quote visibility:

- Account detail shows linked quotes with quote status, approval status, value, latest version and link to quote.
- Opportunity detail shows linked quotes with quote status, approval status, value, latest version and pending approval indicator.
- Users can identify pending quote approvals from CRM account/opportunity detail without opening the quote builder.

## Activity UI

Users must be able to create:

- Call.
- Email.
- Meeting.
- Task.
- Note.

Activities can be completed and linked to account/contact/opportunity.

## CRM Browser Testing Feedback - Sprint 2

### Sprint 2.1 CRM Refinement

Activities:

- Activity UI must support assigning activities to accounts.
- Activity UI must support assigning activities to opportunities.
- Activity UI should optionally support assigning activities to leads and contacts where sensible.
- Account detail pages must show related activities.
- Opportunity and lead detail pages must show related activities.
- Activity rows/cards shown on account, opportunity and lead detail pages must be clickable.

Relationship strength:

- Relationship strength should be a controlled picklist, not numeric or free text.
- Suggested values:
  - Unknown.
  - Weak.
  - Medium.
  - Strong.
  - Secured.
  - Preferred Partner.
  - Only Partner.
- This should apply primarily to contact and account relationship displays/forms.

Opportunity forms:

- Opportunity type must use a controlled picklist.
- Opportunity source must use a controlled picklist.

Account detail:

- Account detail must show all open opportunities linked to the account.
- Linked opportunities must be clickable.
- Account detail must show recent activities.
- Account detail must show key contacts.

Opportunity and lead detail:

- Opportunity detail must show all linked activities.
- Lead detail must show all linked activities.
- Activities must be clickable from both detail views.

### Sprint 3 CRM Pipeline, Forecasting and Automations

Pipeline forecasting UI:

- Pipeline view must show monthly expected close value.
- Pipeline view must show deals expected to close each month.
- Pipeline view must show unweighted pipeline.
- Pipeline view must show weighted pipeline.
- Pipeline view must show pipeline by stage.
- Pipeline view must show pipeline by owner.
- Pipeline reporting values must display in GBP.

Opportunity Kanban:

- Add a Kanban-style pipeline board.
- Opportunities should be displayed in stage columns.
- Drag and drop should support moving opportunities between stage columns.
- Stage columns should use the Connected Hospitality B2B workflow:
  - Lead.
  - Qualified.
  - Pre-Sales / Solution Design.
  - Proposal Sent.
  - Negotiation.
  - Proposal Verbally Accepted.
  - Closed Won (PO Received).
  - Lost.

Currency UI:

- Opportunity forms must allow GBP, EUR and USD.
- Opportunity detail should display original currency/value and GBP converted value.
- Pipeline and forecast screens should always use GBP totals.

Notifications UI:

- CRM notification outputs should use the existing Sprint 1 notification framework.
- Notification rules can be generated by background or scheduled jobs in Sprint 3 or later.

## Sprint 3 Implementation Notes

Implemented Sprint 3 UI scope:

- `/crm/pipeline` shows a Kanban-style opportunity board with Connected Hospitality stage columns.
- `/crm/forecast` groups open opportunities by expected close month.
- CRM Dashboard shows total open pipeline, weighted open pipeline, deals closing this month, due activities, overdue activities and pipeline by stage.
- Activity detail allows open activities to be completed with outcome notes.
- Activity list separates open and completed activities.

Deferred:

- FX conversion and multi-currency opportunity entry.
- Scheduled/background CRM notification jobs.
- Full pipeline by owner reporting.

## Sprint 3.1 Implementation Notes

- Opportunity detail and list views include edit actions.
- `/crm/opportunities/:id/edit` allows CRM users to update opportunity details and stage.
- `/crm/pipeline` supports drag-and-drop stage movement through a client-side Kanban board.
- Kanban moves show visible feedback and refresh pipeline totals after the stage update is saved.
- The drag-and-drop board renders after client mount to avoid dnd-kit hydration ID mismatches.

## Simplified Stage Model

Opportunity stages:

- Lead: 0%.
- Qualified: 10%.
- Pre-Sales / Solution Design: 20%.
- Proposal Sent: 30%.
- Negotiation: 50%.
- Proposal Verbally Accepted: 75%.
- Closed Won (PO Received): 100%.
- Lost: 0%.

## Sales Ownership UI

- The CRM navigation includes `/crm/my-work` for Sales, Administrator and Business Operations users.
- Lead and opportunity lists show the assigned salesperson.
- Opportunity lists also show next action date and expected close date.
- Administrator and Business Operations users see salesperson filters on CRM dashboard, pipeline, forecast, lead list, opportunity list and My Work.
- Sales users do not see the salesperson selector and are shown their own records by default.
- Opportunity create/edit forms show a Salesperson selector only to Administrator and Business Operations users.
- Lead creation shows a Salesperson selector only to Administrator and Business Operations users.

## Sprint 7A Sales Operations UI

- `/crm/my-work` shows My Leads, My Opportunities, My Activities, My Due/Overdue Follow-ups, My Quotes and My Pre-Sales Requests.
- Opportunity health badges appear on opportunity list, opportunity detail, CRM pipeline cards and CRM forecast deal rows.
- Health filters allow users to show Green, Amber and Red opportunity groups on list, dashboard, pipeline and forecast surfaces.
- CRM dashboard includes cards for pipeline value, weighted pipeline, closing this month, closing next month, open pre-sales requests, quotes awaiting approval, overdue opportunities and opportunities requiring attention.
- Quotes list includes Owner and Salesperson columns, a Salesperson filter for Administrator and Business Operations users, and a My Quotes filter.
- The top-right notification bell shows unread count and links to `/notifications`.
